About Beirut Poly

Beirut Poly is a leading polytechnic institute located in the heart of Beirut, Lebanon. We are committed to providing high-quality technical and vocational education that meets the demands of the modern world. Our programs are designed to prepare students for careers in engineering, IT, business, and design.

Our Programs

Engineering

Mechanical, Electrical, and Civil Engineering programs with hands-on labs and industry partnerships.

Information Technology

Software development, networking, and cybersecurity tracks to prepare students for tech-driven careers.
